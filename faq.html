Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Starlight Studio

Starlight Studio is a free, browser-based photobooth that lets you capture photos using your camera or upload existing images. Create beautiful photostrips and postcards with customizable layouts, stickers, captions, and backgrounds. All processed entirely on your device.

Yes! Starlight Studio is completely free. All features are available to everyone.

Starlight Studio works on any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on desktop, tablet, or mobile devices. For camera capture, your device must have a working camera and grant permission to use it.

You can either use your device's camera with a countdown timer (3 seconds), or upload existing photos from your device. The camera option require granting camera permission.

What layouts are available? +

We offer 6 layout options, each layout is designed for different photo arrangements and occasions.

After capturing photos, you can customize with background colors (preset or custom color), stickers, (drag, resize, and rotate), captions with multiple styles, and optional date or location overlay. All customizations happen in real-time on the editor.

Click any stickers to add it to your canvas. Once added, you can drag to reposition, use corner handles to resize, use the rotation handle to rotate, and click the delete button to remove. Stickers work with bot mouse and touch input.

No. Your photos never leave your device. Starlight Studio is fully client-side, all photo capture, editing, and processing happens entirely in your browser. We have no server that receives or stores your images.

We collect no personal data. We don't use cookies, local storage for tracking, or any analytics. The only external request is an optional call to OpenStreetMap to convert your coordinates to a location name and this only happens if you enable the location feature.

Camera permission is optional (only needed for live capture, you can upload photos instead). Geolocation permission is optional (only needed if you want to add location to your photostrip). Both can be denied without affecting core functionality.

For uploads, we support JPEG, PNG, and HEIC formats. For best results, use high-resolution images. Your final photostrip is exported as a high-quality PNG file.

Click the "Download" button in the editor to save your photostrip as a PNG image. The file is generated at high resolution suitable for printing or sharing on social media. The download happens instantly since everything is processed locally.
